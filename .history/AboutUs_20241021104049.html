Our story began with the vision of harnessing the power of technology to transform Power, Gas and Water Utilities management capabilities and lives connected with it. For two decades, Inventive has stood out because of its innovative technology, and ambitious projects, starting from CMRI-based billing of consumers to smart metering solutions provider in the technologically connected world. We offer disruptive technology to support Power, Gas and Water Utilities across the World.

Inventive is trusted to encompass utility transformation challenges that are fueled by the fast evolving and innovative ecosystem of cloud data, connectivity, software and people
